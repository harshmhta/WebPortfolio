fun facts

Hi there! I'm Harsh, a creative and innovative front-end developer based in the city of Arlington. As a recent graduate of Penn State with a degree in Computer Science and a minor in Entrepreneurship & Innovation, I have a deep understanding of both the technical and business aspects of web development.

My passion for creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites led me to specialize in front-end development, where I've honed my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I believe that great design and user experience are crucial for the success of any website, and I strive to create seamless and intuitive designs that engage users and drive business growth.

In my time at Penn State, I took advantage of opportunities to work on real-world projects and collaborate with fellow students and entrepreneurs. Through these experiences, I gained valuable insights into project management, problem-solving, and effective communication.

What sets me apart as a front-end developer is my ability to understand and incorporate a client's unique brand identity into their website design. I approach each new project with curiosity and a desire to understand the vision, so I can create a website that not only looks great but also accurately represents their brand and message.

Outside of work, I love exploring the outdoors and trying new things. Whether it's hiking a new trail or trying a new cuisine, I'm always up for an adventure. I also enjoy attending tech meetups and conferences to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques in front-end development.

I'm excited to continue pushing my creative boundaries and collaborating with clients to create stunning and effective websites. Let's connect and see how we can bring your vision to life!

manifesto

I believe in the power of creativity and innovation to make a positive impact on the world

I believe that technology can be a powerful tool for positive change, but only if we approach it with empathy and a sense of responsibility. It's not enough to create flashy websites or apps that serve no real purpose; we must think critically about the impact that our work has on the world.

I believe in the importance of accessibility, both in terms of design and functionality. The internet is a vital resource for people all over the world, and we must ensure that our websites and applications are usable by as many people as possible, regardless of their abilities.

I believe that collaboration and communication are key to success in any project. By working closely with my clients, stakeholders, and team members, I can create solutions that truly meet their needs and exceed their expectations.

I believe that innovation and experimentation are crucial to staying at the forefront of this ever-evolving industry. I will never stop learning, growing, and exploring new technologies and techniques to improve my craft.

Above all, I believe that our work as front-end web developers has the power to make a difference in the world. By using our skills and creativity to solve real problems and create meaningful experiences, we can shape the digital landscape for the better.

So I pledge to always strive for excellence, to never settle for mediocrity, and to never forget the impact that our work can have on the world around us. Let's create a better web, together.
